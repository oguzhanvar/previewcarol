Divorce Solutions

"Guiding You Through the Challenges of Divorce with Compassion and Expertise"

Divorce is a legal procedure that dissolves a marriage, involving these key steps:
Filing the Petition:
One spouse files a divorce petition, citing reasons for the dissolution.
Service of Process:
The other spouse is served with the divorce papers.
Response and Counterpetition:
The respondent has a set time to respond and may file a counterpetition.
Discovery and Disclosure:
Both parties disclose financial information, ensuring transparency.
Negotiation and Settlement:
Many couples opt for negotiation and mediation to reach an agreement on asset division, support, and custody.
Court Proceedings and Trial:
If no agreement is reached, the divorce may proceed to trial.
Finalization of Divorce Decree:
Once the court approves the settlement or issues a final judgment, a divorce decree is issued, officially terminating the marriage.
